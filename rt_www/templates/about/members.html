Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I purchase a swim pass?

A: You can purchase a swim pass or other Red Tide merchandise directly from your account page. Just log in and click on “Order Now” under Club Links on the main account page. The Swim Pass is listed under the Merchandise page. Follow directions to check out.

Q: How do I switch change my fee option or cancel my membership?

A: Please email billing@redtidenyc.org for all inquiries and requests relating to your account.
